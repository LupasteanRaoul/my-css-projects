## ğŸš€ **How to Use**

### **Basic Usage**
1. Fill in your name, email, and message
2. Select a subject from the dropdown
3. Agree to the privacy policy
4. Click "Send Message" or press Ctrl+Enter

### **Keyboard Shortcuts**
- **Ctrl + Enter**: Submit form
- **Escape**: Reset form
- **Tab**: Navigate between fields

### **Form Features**
- **Auto-save**: Form data is saved automatically
- **Character counter**: Shows message length (max 500 chars)
- **Reset**: Clear all fields with confirmation
- **Multiple submissions**: Send another message after success
